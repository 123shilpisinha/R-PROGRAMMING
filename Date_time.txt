Date are represented by date class
Time are represented by POXISct or POIXlt class
as.Date()----converting into date
as.POSIXct()  or as.POSIXlt----converting into time
POSIXct---stores large integer
POSIXlt---stores list
We can perform operation such as +,-,*,/ only when both time are of same type.
Format of strptime---%b %d ,%y %h %m     represents month,day,year,hour and minutes.
