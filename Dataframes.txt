Used to store tabuler data
special type of list where every element of list has to have the same length
can store different classes of objects
created by calling---read.csv() or read.table()
can be converted into matrix as---data.matrix
FOr eg--->x<-data_frame(a=1:4,b=c(T,T,F,F))
>x
a  b
1  T
2  T
3  F
4  F
